Introduction

Our company was established in 1993 as a family company. In the beginning we were making cable soldiering, but as the time went on we had to be open for the new challenges of the market. This was the reason we started growing and the form of our company changed, in 2005 our new company, the Andri-Tech Ltd. was established.

Main profiles

Manufacture of repaired cables and wires
Industrial soldiering.
Quality controlling.
Assembly of sensitive metal or plastic products by hand.
We offer telecommunicating and entertaining tools’ repair. In the case the commodity, half-done or done product is damaged we undertake 100% selection and reparation according to instructions that can take place in the customer’s location or in our premise. We give full and permanent particulars of the work we made in case of the customer’s need. Our group is also competent in repairing big amount of mechanic and electronic components that can be done by the customer’s need or according to our strategy.
